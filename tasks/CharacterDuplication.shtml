This task tests your ability to draw a character, which you have likely never seen before, from memory. Do not worry if you can't duplicate the character exactly; just try your best.

This task tests your ability to hallucinate a character that is similar to examples in a particular group. You will be shown 2 different images of each of 2 characters from the same group of characters. You will be asked to draw another, original, character that you think could belong in the same group.

For example, if you were shown some examples of things that looked like Chinese characters, your drawing should look something like a Chinese character, and should not be identical to any of the characters you were shown.

Each subtask has the following structure:

  1. A red square appears to cue your attention.
  2. The red square disappears and a green square appears in the same place to to cue your attention.
  3. Shortly after the cue, the example character is quickly flashed in that square.
  4. After the flash, the character disappears and is replaced by a nonsense scribble image.
