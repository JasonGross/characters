Instructions

Below you will see examples of handwritten characters from many different scripts, most of which you will not recognize. Underneath each set of alphabets is one character that is not grouped with any alphabet. Your job is to categorize this character, to select which of the ten alphabets above it it is most likely to be from. Indicate which alphabet you think the extra character is from in one of three ways. You can:

  • click on the appropriate alphabet, OR
  • click on the drop-down menu and select an alphabet number (alphabet numbers are on top of the corresponding examples), OR
  • drag and drop the character on top of one of the alphabets.

Please note:
  • You may change any of your answers at any time before submission.
  • You must categorize every non-example character to submit the form. (The questions at the bottom, however, are optional.)
    • If you somehow manage to submit the form without categorizing every character, your submission will be rejected.
  • So long as you categorize every non-example character, and you make an effort to categorize them correctly, your submission will be accepted independent of how many you get correct.
